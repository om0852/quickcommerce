# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### Q: What is QuickCommerce Tracker?

A: QuickCommerce Tracker is a real-time price monitoring and analytics platform for India's quick-commerce sector. It tracks product prices, availability, and trends across 6 major platforms: Zepto, Blinkit, JioMart, DMart, Flipkart Minutes, and Swiggy Instamart.

### Q: Is QuickCommerce Tracker free?

A: We offer a **Free plan** with basic features. Premium plans include advanced analytics, API access, and more features. See [Pricing](/pricing) for details.

### Q: Which regions does QuickCommerce Tracker cover?

A: We cover major metropolitan areas and cities across India where quick-commerce services operate. New regions are added regularly.

### Q: How often is price data updated?

A: Price data is updated 2-4 times daily depending on the platform. Zepto and Blinkit data updates every few hours. Check the "Last Updated" timestamp for the most recent update time.

## Account & Access

### Q: How do I create an account?

A: Click **Sign Up** on the homepage, enter your email, create a password, and verify your email. You'll get immediate access to the Free tier.

### Q: Can I reset my password?

A: Yes! Click **Forgot Password** on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions sent to your email.

### Q: Can I have multiple accounts?

A: Yes, you can create multiple accounts using different emails. Each account has separate alerts and watchlists.

### Q: Is my data secure?

A: Absolutely. We use 256-bit SSL encryption and follow GDPR/data protection regulations. Your data is never shared with third parties.

## Features & Functionality

### Q: How do I set up price alerts?

A: Go to any product, click the bell icon, set your target price, choose notification method, and save. You'll be notified when the price drops to your target.

### Q: How far back is historical data available?

A: Historical price data is available for the past 1 year. Older data can be archived or deleted based on plan.

### Q: Can I export my data?

A: Yes! Click **Export** to download data in CSV, JSON, or Excel format. Premium plans offer automated scheduled exports.

### Q: Can I share alerts with my team?

A: In Premium plans, you can share watchlists and create team accounts with role-based access.

### Q: How do I compare prices across platforms?

A: Use the dashboard's built-in comparison table. Select platforms you want to compare, and prices are shown side-by-side.

## Data & Analytics

### Q: Why do prices differ across platforms?

A: Different platforms use different pricing strategies, regional costs, and supplier arrangements. Prices often vary by ₹5-₹20 on the same product.

### Q: How is the "Price Index" calculated?

A: It's a normalized price indicator where 100 = baseline price. Index 110 means 10% above baseline, 90 means 10% below.

### Q: Can I predict future prices?

A: Premium plans include price forecasting based on historical trends and machine learning models.

## Troubleshooting

### Q: Why aren't my alerts triggering?

**Check:**
- Alert is still active (not paused)
- Price target is realistic
- Product is still being tracked
- Email is not filtered as spam

### Q: Why is data showing as "Last Updated" > 2 hours ago?

A: This might indicate:
- Platform is temporarily unavailable
- Network issue accessing platform
- Platform changed their site structure

Our team works to restore service quickly. Check [Status Page](/status) for updates.

### Q: Why are prices different from what I see on the app?

A: Possible reasons:
- Different geographical location service
- Personalized pricing based on your account
- App data not yet refreshed
- Our data snapshot captured moments before your check

Refresh the page to get the latest update.

### Q: Can I track products without signing up?

A: No, you need an account to use QuickCommerce Tracker. Creating an account is free and takes 2 minutes!
