# Welcome to QuickCommerce Tracker

Monitor and analyze product prices, availability, and trends across India's leading quick-commerce platforms in real-time.

## Supported Platforms

Track prices and availability on:
- **Zepto** - Fast commerce delivery
- **Blinkit** - Instant grocery delivery
- **JioMart** - Reliance's quick-commerce
- **DMart** - Retail chain quick-commerce
- **Flipkart Minutes** - Flipkart's quick-commerce service
- **Swiggy Instamart** - Swiggy's grocery delivery

Get up and running in 3 steps:

  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Category & Region">
